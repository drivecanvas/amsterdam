Our typeface is Avenir by Linotype.

It is a default system font on Apple devices. If you're editing a document, you can simply select Avenir from your fonts.

On the web, we serve the typeface through fonts.com

Font Scale

Our typography is based on a base scale of 1rem / 16px.

    16 x .875= 14
    16 x 1   = 16
    16 x 1.25= 20
    16 x 1.5 = 24
    16 x 2   = 32
    16 x 3   = 48
    16 x 4   = 64
